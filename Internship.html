About YSSE

Youth School for Social Entrepreneurs (YSSE) is a socially volunteered youth-led non-profit organization, working for social development since 2015.

YSSE is dedicated to eradicating extreme poverty and solving youth unemployment by empowering youth and women through promoting social entrepreneurship.

YSSE has expanded across 32+ countries, organizing awareness programs, global youth summits, and impactful social initiatives.

Program Overview & Specifics

YSSE offers virtual internship opportunities for Bangladeshi university and college students who are proficient in English and Bengali. This is an opportunity to learn corporate culture, build skills, and contribute to meaningful change.

Applicants may choose ONE department from the list below based on their proficiency and interest:

  • Admin and HR
  • Business Development
  • Communication
  • Content Writing
  • Graphic Design
  • Marketing & PR
  • Operations Management
  • Video Editing
